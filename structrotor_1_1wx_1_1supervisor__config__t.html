wxEvtHandler* rotor::wx::supervisor_config_t::handler

the wx context, responsible for messages delivery

Actuall rotor-message delivery for actors runnion on the top of wx-loop is performed via wx-events, i.e. rotor-messages are wrapped into wx-events.

The wx-supervisor subscribes to the wx-events and unswraps the rotor-messages from the events.

The wx event handler is used as a transport medium
