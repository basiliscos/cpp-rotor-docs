Detailed Description

template<typename Actor, typename Handler, typename ErrHandler>
struct rotor::asio::forwarder_t< Actor, Handler, ErrHandler >

dispatches single boost::asio callback with error_code and non-error results into two differnt functions of the actor. After the invocation, actor's supervisor do_process method is called to process message queue.

The invocation is strand-aware.
